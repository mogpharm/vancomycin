Future Dose Regimen Estimator
Select a proposed dose and interval to estimate steady-state PK.
Model: One-compartment IV infusion, linear superposition.
During infusion: C(t) = (R/Vk)(1−e−kΔt) where R = dose/tinf  ·  After infusion: C(t) = C(tinf) × e−k(t−tinf)
k (Matzke): k = 0.00083×CrCl + 0.0044  ·  Vd: 0.7×TBW  ·  CrCl (Cockcroft–Gault): ((140−age)×wt)/(72×SCrmg/dL) × 0.85 if ♀
2-level solver: Bisection on C₂·N(k,t₁) = C₁·N(k,t₂) — handles doses between levels. SS regimen: Ctrough,ss = (R/Vk)(1−e−k·tinf)·e−k(τ−tinf)/(1−e−kτ)
